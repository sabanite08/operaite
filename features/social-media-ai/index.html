Questions

Frequently asked.

Which platforms does it write for?
Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, X (Twitter), and TikTok captions. Each platform has its own conventions — LinkedIn captions are longer and professional, TikTok captions are short and hook-driven, Instagram is somewhere in between.
Can it write in my voice?
Yes. Set your business voice and a 1-paragraph description of your audience in your business profile. The AI inherits both into every generation.
Does it generate hashtags too?
Yes — 10 to 15 hashtags per caption, mixed between high-volume and niche tags. You can ask for industry-specific hashtags or local tags too.
Does it post for me?
No — the AI generates the caption and hashtags; you copy and paste into Instagram/LinkedIn/Facebook. We don't connect to platforms because their API access is restrictive.
What if I have nothing to post about?
Generate post ideas from your business profile — the AI suggests 10 post ideas at a time. From there, pick one and generate the full caption.
Is this a free tool or paid?
Social Media AI is part of the paid Operaite app ($29/mo, 7-day free trial). Unlimited captions and hashtags.
