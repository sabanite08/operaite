Questions

Frequently asked.

What goes into the generated proposal?
Scope of work, deliverables, timeline (with milestones), pricing breakdown, payment terms, and an optional next-steps section.
Does it pull from my business profile?
Yes. Your business name, logo, contact info, currency, and tone preference all auto-fill.
Can I edit the proposal after the AI generates it?
Yes — the proposal is editable text after generation. Tweak the language, adjust pricing, swap milestones, then download or send.
How does this compare to Bonsai or HoneyBook?
Bonsai and HoneyBook focus on the full freelance OS — contracts, e-signatures, time tracking. Operaite is broader and cheaper, with the proposal generator as one of 13 AI tools.
Can the client sign it?
E-signature isn't built in yet. Most owners send the PDF and get a "looks good, let's start" email back. Formal e-signature is on the roadmap.
Is this a free tool or paid?
Client Proposals is part of the paid Operaite app ($29/mo, 7-day free trial). Unlimited generations.
