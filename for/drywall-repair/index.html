Questions

Frequently asked.

How do I price drywall patches vs full-room work?
Patches: per-patch flat fee ($75–$200 each, larger patches over 4 sq ft at $150–$350). Rooms or full installs: per sheet ($45–$80/sheet installed, including tape and mud). Texture matching adds $40–$100 per area depending on texture type.
Should I include paint touch-up in my drywall quote?
Two paths: (1) primer-ready finish only with explicit “paint by others” note for clean handoff to the painter. Or (2) include paint touch-up if you’re licensed/insured and the job is small (one wall, matching existing). Spell out which path on every quote.
How do I handle texture-matching for older homes?
Quote texture matching as a separate line item with disclaimer: “best-effort match; older textures may not be perfectly replicable.” Knockdown, orange peel, and popcorn typically match well; hand-trowel, swirl, and skip-trowel are harder. Note in the proposal which you can match cleanly.
Can it handle insurance claim work?
Yes — invoices and proposals include insurance-friendly itemization with claim/policy numbers in the reference field. Many drywall repair jobs come from water damage and need detailed line items for the adjuster.
