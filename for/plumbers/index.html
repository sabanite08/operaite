Questions

Frequently asked.

Can it handle emergency and weekend rates?
Yes — line items support different rates, and the AI is good at writing notes that explain emergency surcharges without it feeling like a fight.
Does it handle T&M for water-line repairs?
Yes. Most plumbers structure these as hourly labor, materials at cost-plus, and rental equipment as a third line.
What about commercial / property-management billing?
Multiple property addresses for the same client work fine — invoice has separate fields for client and service location.
Is the review responder good for 1-star angry reviews?
Especially good for those — plumbing customers are often stressed. The AI acknowledges, takes responsibility without admitting legal fault, and offers to make it right off-platform.
