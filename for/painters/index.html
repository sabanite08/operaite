Questions

Frequently asked.

Does the proposal generator handle surface prep + paint separately?
Yes — most painters structure proposals as prep + primer + paint sections. The AI organizes it cleanly so customers see the actual work.
Can it handle sq-ft based pricing?
Yes — line items can be qty × rate where qty is square footage. The invoice displays the calculation clearly.
What about exterior jobs that depend on weather?
The scheduling tool lets you reschedule with a single click. AI is good at writing the "we have to push your job a few days due to rain" email.
Does it integrate with PaintScout or Estimate Rocket?
No direct integrations. Most painting owners use a measurement app for sq-footage and Operaite for the rest.
