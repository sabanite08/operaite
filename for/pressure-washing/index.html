Questions

Frequently asked.

Should I price by sq ft or flat rate?
Sq ft for predictable surfaces (driveways, walkways, patios) at $0.20–$0.40/sq ft. Flat rate for variable surfaces (full house exteriors, decks, fences) where time depends on grime level more than area. Most pressure washers use sq ft on the quote and adjust on site if the actual condition differs significantly.
How do I price soft wash separately from power wash?
Soft wash (low PSI + sodium hypochlorite for siding, roofs, painted surfaces) typically commands a 30–50% premium over straight power wash. List as separate line items on the invoice so customers see the right method matched to the right surface — and so you justify the chemical cost.
Can it handle weather-related rescheduling?
Yes — bulk-reschedule when forecasts change. Cancellation policy is built into booking (“weather cancellations are free reschedules; same-day customer cancellations have a fee”).
How does it handle annual / recurring contracts?
Annual maintenance (twice-yearly siding wash, quarterly commercial property cleaning) works as recurring schedule entries. Each scheduled visit auto-generates an invoice with the contract pricing.
