Questions

Frequently asked.

Is Jobber worth $79/mo?
For multi-truck operations, yes — route optimization and dispatching pay for themselves. For solo operators, you’re paying for depth you won’t use.
Why would I pick Operaite over Jobber?
Price ($29 vs $79), AI tools Jobber doesn’t have (review responder, marketing audit, social writer), and simplicity for solo operators.
Can I switch from Jobber to Operaite?
No direct import. Export client list as CSV from Jobber and re-add manually. Solo operators report 2-3 hours to migrate basic client info.
Does Operaite have GPS / route tracking?
No — Jobber wins clearly here. If GPS dispatch is core to how you run, use Jobber.
