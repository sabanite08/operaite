Questions

Frequently asked.

Is Housecall Pro worth $69/mo?
For established HVAC/plumbing/electrical with QuickBooks as a requirement, yes. For solo operators, you’re paying for depth you won’t use.
Why would I pick Operaite over Housecall Pro?
Price ($29 vs $69), AI tools HCP doesn’t have (review responder, marketing audit, social writer), faster setup.
Can I switch from Housecall Pro to Operaite?
No direct import. CSV export of clients works; equipment-history records won’t transfer. Factor that in if you rely on equipment tracking.
Does Operaite integrate with QuickBooks?
No. Operaite has simple income/expense tracking but no QB sync. If your accountant requires QB, HCP is the right answer.
