Cliff notes

Who each is for.

🚚
Jobber
2-10 truck service businesses with growing dispatching needs. Mature drag-drop scheduling, native tech mobile app, customer hub. The standard for established trades. $79+/mo.
🏠
Housecall Pro
HVAC, plumbing, electrical businesses with QuickBooks as a hard requirement and equipment-history needs (service contracts). $69+/mo.
🏢
ServiceTitan
$1M+ revenue service businesses with multi-truck operations, dispatchers, consumer financing integration. Enterprise pricing ($400+/mo to $2,000+/mo loaded), 30-60 day implementation. The standard at scale.
🔮
Operaite
Owner-operators in trades and services. AI tools (review responder, marketing audit, social writer) bundled with basic invoicing for $29/mo. Self-serve, no rep, 10-minute setup. Lighter on dispatching, deeper on AI.

Honest about where we don’t compete.

If you need GPS dispatching for 5+ trucks, use Jobber. If you need QuickBooks sync + HVAC equipment tracking, use Housecall Pro. If you’re doing $1M+ with a real back office, use ServiceTitan. Otherwise, Operaite at $29/mo is a fair option.
